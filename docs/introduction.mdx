BBC is **the company brain**. It captures the knowledge your team has but never wrote down, structures it as typed memory, and hands it to role-scoped agents that turn it into work.

It's free, open-source (AGPLv3), and self-hostable. Bring your own model API keys.

## What you actually get

- **Loop 1 — Ingest.** Paste a brain-dump. BBC extracts typed memories (decisions, voice, vendors, product positioning, team) and asks you to review them before they land.
- **Loop 2 — Act.** Role-scoped agents (Marketing Studio, Engineering Studio, more coming) read the brain and produce work in your voice with citations back to the memories that shaped each sentence.
- **Loop 3 — Improve.** BBC watches how the team uses the brain and files improvement proposals back into the same queue. (Designed, not yet built.)

## What BBC is *not*

- Not a vector store. The brain is typed, queryable, and human-readable — not a blob of embeddings.
- Not a SaaS. There's no paywall and no Stripe wired up. Run it yourself or use the hosted demo.
- Not a chatbot. The agents produce specific, reviewable artifacts (drafts, ADRs, proposals), not chat threads.
