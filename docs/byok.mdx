---
title: "Bring your own keys"
description: "Wire your own model provider keys per tenant. BBC stores them encrypted; no central billing."
---

BBC is BYOK by design. There is no central credit metering and no managed billing — every LLM call uses keys the tenant provided.

## Why BYOK

- **No metering surprises.** You see your Anthropic console; we don't sit between you and your provider.
- **No central liability.** BBC's host (yours, or the demo) never sees your keys after they're encrypted.
- **Self-host friendly.** A single-person self-host puts the key in `.env.local` and never touches the per-tenant flow.

## Per-tenant setup

After signing in, go to `/settings/keys`.

1. Paste your Anthropic API key (starts with `sk-ant-`).
2. Click **Save**. The key is encrypted with AES-256-GCM using `BBC_SECRET_ENCRYPTION_KEY` and stored in the `tenant_keys` table.
3. Studio runs from that tenant now bill against your account.

## Fallback

If a tenant has no key on file, BBC falls back to the host's `ANTHROPIC_API_KEY` env var. The demo deployment caps this fallback with a soft daily limit; self-host deployments don't.

## Rotation

Re-save a key at `/settings/keys` to overwrite the encrypted row. Old runs keep their cost attribution metadata; new runs use the new key.

## Other providers

The provider catalog is in `memory/ops/providers/*.yaml` (file-mode) and the `provider_adapters` table (DB-mode). Add a row, declare what role it `implements`, and `/bindings` lets you switch the active provider for that role. See [role-tool-bundle](/concepts/role-tool-bundle).
